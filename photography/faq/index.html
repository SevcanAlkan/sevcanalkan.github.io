FAQ

Short answers to the questions most likely to come up around my photography, image usage, and print requests.

Common Questions

Can I repost one of your photos?

Please ask first. Reposting to social media, websites, or forums requires permission.

Is credit enough to use a photo?

No. Credit is required when permission is granted, but credit alone is not permission.

Do you sell prints?

Open print sales are not active yet, but you can still email me about a specific print request.

Can I request a higher-resolution file?

Sometimes. It depends on the photo, the intended use, and whether permission is granted.

Where are you based?

Sofia, Bulgaria, with a strong focus on aviation photography and sports photography.

What do you usually shoot with?

The current public setup is Nikon Z8, Nikkor Z 180-600, Fujifilm X-T5, and XF 50-140.

Where can I see more of your photos?

Flickr is the main public archive, and a curated favorites page is being built here.
