Quick Rules

  • Linking to the original Flickr or social post is fine.
  • Personal use is permitted without authorization.
  • Commercial use requires prior written permission from the copyright holder.
  • If you are unsure whether a use is personal or commercial, contact me first.
  • Credit is appreciated, but credit alone does not replace permission when permission is required.
  • Please do not remove watermarks, metadata, or ownership information where present.

What To Include In A Request

  • A link to the specific photo
  • Where the image will appear
  • Whether the usage is personal, editorial, or commercial
  • The format: web, print, social, article, presentation, or other
  • Any deadline or publication date
