Product vision

Pet care breaks down quickly when vaccines, medication, meals, appointments, allergies, and emergency details live across memory, chats, paper notes, and disconnected apps. Shylo brings that information into one place built around clarity, daily use, and confidence during busy moments.

The goal is a calm product that helps owners stay organized every day and makes care handoff easier when somebody else needs to step in.

Features

  • Pet profiles with important health and care details
  • Vaccines, treatments, and medication records
  • Pill tracking with reminders and completion logs
  • Meal tracking with feeding times and notes
  • Calendar views for upcoming, overdue, and completed care
  • Widgets for next due items and daily checklists
  • Document and photo attachments
  • A unified care timeline for routines, records, and history
All Features
  • Pet profiles with name, species, breed, age, weight, allergies, chronic conditions, microchip ID, vet contact, and notes
  • Vaccines, treatments, medications, parasite care, and other health records
  • Medication and pill tracking with dosage, recurrence, completion, and skipped logs
  • Meal tracking with feeding times, portions, food type, and appetite notes
  • Calendar views for upcoming, past, completed, and overdue events
  • Reminders and notifications for vaccines, pills, treatments, appointments, and routines
  • Widgets for next due items, daily care checklists, medication status, and pet summaries
  • Attachments and documents such as vaccine cards, prescriptions, invoices, and photos
  • A unified care timeline that combines medical events, routines, and logged activity
  • Later additions such as symptom journal, weight tracking, multi-pet support, family sharing, Caregiver Mode, nearby clinics and pet shops, and lightweight Apple AI conveniences
