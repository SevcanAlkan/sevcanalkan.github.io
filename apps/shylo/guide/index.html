User Guide

A simple starter guide for using Shylo. This page is a first documentation route and can grow into fuller help content later.

Overview

Shylo helps keep pet care information in one place, including records, reminders, meals, medications, and routine history.

Getting started

  1. Create your pet profile with the most important care details.
  2. Add vaccines, treatments, medications, and feeding routines.
  3. Turn on reminders for upcoming care tasks.
  4. Use the timeline to review what was done and what is due next.

Pet profile

Start by entering the pet name and any useful health or care information, such as allergies, chronic conditions, vet contact details, or notes you want available during daily care.

Records and reminders

Add vaccines, treatments, medication schedules, and meals as individual care items. Once reminders are configured, Shylo helps you keep upcoming work visible and easier to complete on time.

Documents and photos

Keep supporting files such as vaccine cards, prescriptions, invoices, and reference photos attached to the pet record when those features are available in the app.
