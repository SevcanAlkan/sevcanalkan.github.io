Product vision

Wordminder is designed for people who want a fast, personal vocabulary workflow instead of a crowded language-learning system. The focus is on collecting useful words, reviewing them simply, and turning them into a steady daily habit.

The goal is a calm study tool that keeps vocabulary capture and practice easy enough to repeat every day.

Features

  • Personal vocabulary collections by language pair
  • Words with translations, meanings, difficulty, and proficiency labels
  • Flashcard-style multiple-choice practice
  • Daily reminder flow for consistent study
  • Practice counts and success rates for words and collections
  • Predefined starter collections
  • Theme and language preferences
  • Cloud-backed sync for collections, words, and practice stats
